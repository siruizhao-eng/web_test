Journey Through the Lands Between

These are the legendary enemies, impossible trials, and personal victories that shaped my journey as a Tarnished. Every quest is more than a mission. It is a test of patience, timing, courage, and the will to continue even after defeat.

Stormveil Castle

The first major fortress on the path to greatness. Facing Margit and Godrick taught the Tarnished that power alone is not enough. Victory comes from learning patterns, staying calm, and striking at the right moment.

Starscourge Radahn

A battle beneath a blood-red sky against the conqueror of the stars. This fight represents chaos, scale, and courage. The Tarnished rides into war with allies, dodging arrows, magic, and the force of a broken legend.

Malenia, Blade of Miquella

The ultimate test of discipline. Malenia is not simply defeated through strength. She demands precision, patience, and the ability to recover from failure again and again.

The Erdtree

The final destination of the Tarnished. After countless battles, lost runes, discovered secrets, and broken enemies, the warrior stands before destiny and reaches for the title of Elden Lord.

Lore

Rise, Tarnished

In the shattered Lands Between, the Tarnished walks beneath the fading light of grace. Banished long ago and forgotten by the Golden Order, the warrior rises once more to seek the fragments of the Elden Ring and claim the throne of Elden Lord.

Across ruined castles, poisoned swamps, underground cities, and battlefields soaked in blood, the Tarnished faces enemies that seem impossible. Demigods stand in the way. Ancient monsters guard forgotten places. Every step forward requires courage, patience, and the willingness to fall and rise again.

This website is designed as an Elden Ring themed portfolio. Instead of a normal personal homepage, it presents the journey like a dark fantasy legend. The sections are changed into Lore, Build, Quests, and Summon to match the feeling of the game world.
